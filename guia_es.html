Ultimate Technical Minecraft Guide

Step 1: Starting Out

Start freely. You don't need full armor yet—basic stone tools are enough. Look for a village.

Step 2: Villager Trading

Use sticks (crafted from wood) to trade with fletchers and get emeralds. Level up villagers even if you "waste" emeralds at first.

The best food is golden carrots (from farmer villagers).

Step 3: Iron Farm

Build a semi-automatic or automatic iron farm. Keep at least 100 blocks distance from the villager breeder for max efficiency.

Step 4: Giant Tree Harvest

Spruce trees (4x4) yield lots of wood. Great for trading and crafting.

Step 5: End and Elytra

Prepare with full diamond armor (from villagers, even if it's not perfect). Bring feather falling potions to fight the Ender Dragon safely.

Step 6: XP Farm

Build an XP farm using villagers for enchantments. It's easy and very efficient.
