Minecraft PRO Technical Guide

1. Starting your technical world

Start by collecting wood and stone, crafting basic tools, and locating a village.

2. Trading with villagers

Villagers are essential. Begin trading sticks with the Fletcher. Level them up to access enchanted gear, tools and books.

Tip: The best food source is golden carrots, offered by farmers.

3. Iron and Villager Farms

While trading, build an iron farm. Keep it at least 100 blocks away from the villager breeder for max efficiency.

You can use this semi-automatic breeder or go fully automatic for mass production.

4. Endgame progress

With full diamond gear from villagers, even if low enchantments, go defeat the Ender Dragon. Feather falling potions are useful against knockback.

Then collect your Elytra and start your farms and mega builds!
